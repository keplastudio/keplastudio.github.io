Privacy Policy — Pasteboard

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

Pasteboard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All clipboard content you capture stays exclusively on your device.

2. On-Device Storage

Everything Pasteboard saves — text clips, images, categories, and settings — is stored locally in a private database accessible only by the app. No data is sent to any server or third party.

3. Encryption

Content stored in private categories is encrypted using AES-128 (CBC/PKCS5Padding) before being written to the local database. Access requires biometric authentication (fingerprint or device unlock).

4. Permissions

Pasteboard may request the following permissions:

  • Accessibility Service — to detect clipboard changes and auto-save copied content. Optional; the app works manually without it.
  • Internet — used exclusively for Google Play Billing to verify the Pro license. No other data is transmitted.

5. In-App Purchases

The Pro upgrade is processed through Google Play Billing. Kepla Studio does not receive or store any payment information. Google's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

6. Third Parties

Pasteboard does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete individual clips, categories, or all app data at any time from within the app. When deleted through the app, data is permanently removed from the local database immediately.

Pasteboard uses Android Auto Backup, which may store app data in your personal Google Drive account to restore after reinstallation. This backup is encrypted, tied to your Google account, and is never accessible to Kepla Studio. To fully remove all data, uninstall the app and delete the associated backup from your Google Drive (Settings → Google → Backup).

Manual backup files created through the app's export feature are stored on-device and must be deleted separately by the user.

8. GDPR

As Pasteboard does not collect personal data, GDPR obligations are minimal. EU users retain the right to request deletion of any locally stored data by uninstalling the app and removing the Android backup as described above.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at keplastudio@gmail.com.
